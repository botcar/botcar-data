1. Anguis Gracilis Maculatus: The Spotted Ribbon-Snake.
    Pseudo-Santalum croceum: Brasiletto.
    Flos Passionis minimus, trilobatus flore sub-caeruleo.

  2. Anguis Annulatus: The Chain-Snake.
    Frutex Lauri longiore folio.

  3. Anguis Viridis Maculatus: The Green Spotted Snake.
    Apocynum Scandens, Salicis folio, flore amplo pleno.

  4. Anguis Flagelli Formis: The Coach-whip Snake.
    Lychnis vicosa, Virginiana, flore amplo coccineo: Seu Muscipula Regia.

  5. Anguis e rubro & albo various: The Corn Snake.
    Viscum Caryophylloides ramosum, floribus minimis albis.

  6. Anguis Capite Viperino: The Hog-nose Snake.

    Lilium, sive Martagon Canadense, flore luteo punctato.

  7. Anguis Viridis: The Green Snake.
    Cassena vera Floridanorum, Arbuscula baccifera Alaternifacie, foliis alternatim sitis, tetrapyrene.

  8. Anguis e caeruluo & albo varius: The Wampum Snake.
    Lilium Carolinianum, flore croceo punctato, petalis longioribus & angustioribus: The Red Lilly.

  9. Caecilia Maculata: The Glass Snake.
    Chryanthemum Americanum, Doronici folio, flore Persici coloris, umbone magno prominente ex atro purpureo, viridi, & aureo fulgente.

  10. Anguis niger, maculis rubris & luteis eleganter varius: The Bead Snake.
    Convolvulus Radice tuberoso esculento: The Virginian Potato.

  11. Magnolia altissima, flore ingenti candido: The Laurel Tree of Carolina.

  12. Putorius Americanus Striatus: The Pol-Cat.
    Pseudo-Phalangium ramosum.

  13. Lacertus omnium maximus, Crocodilus: The Alligator.
    Candela Americana, foliis Laurinis, flore tetrapetalo luteo, fructu angustiore: The Mangrove Tree.

  14. Lacertus Indicus, Senembi & Inguana dictus: The Guana.
    Anona maxima, foliis latis fructu maximo luteo conoide, cortice glabro.

  15. Lacertus Viridis Carolinensis: The Green Lizard of Carolina.
    Liquid-Ambari Arbor, Seu Styraciflua, Aceris folio, fructu Tribuloide, i.e. Pericarpio orbiculari ex quam plurimus apicibus coagmentato, semen recondens: The Sweet Gum-Tree.

  16. Lacertus Viridis Jamaicensis: The Green Lizard of Jamaica.
    Lignum Campechianum; species quaedam Brasil: Logwood.

  17. Lacertus Cauda Caerulea: The Blue-Tailed Lizard.
    Anona fructu viridi laevi, Pyri inversi forma.

  18. Lacertus Griseus: The Lyon Lizard.
    Viscum Caryophylloides, foliis longis in apice incisis, floris labello alto trifido, petalis luteis, longis angustissimis.

  19. Ranaterrestris: The Land Frog.
    Sarracena, foliis longioribus & angustioribus; Bucanephyllon elatius Virginianum.

  20. Rana Aquatica: The Water Frog.
    Sarracena, foliis brevioribus latioribus Sarracena Canadensis, foliis cavis & auritis.

  21. Rana Viridis Arborea: The Green Tree Frog.
    Arum Americanum, Betae folio: The Scunk Weed.

  22. Rana maxima Americana Aquatica: The Bull Frog.
    Helleborine: The Lady's Slipper of Pensilvania.

  23. Scuriurus Niger: The Black Squirrel.
    Calceolus Marianus glaber, Petalis angustis: The Yellow Lady's Slipper.

  24. ASciurus Virginianus cinereus major: The Gray Fox Squirrel.
    Viscum Caryophilloides, floribus parvis luteis punctatis.

  25. Sciurus Striatus: The Ground Squirrel.
    Cornus, foliis Laurinis, fructu majore luteo: The Mastic Tree.
