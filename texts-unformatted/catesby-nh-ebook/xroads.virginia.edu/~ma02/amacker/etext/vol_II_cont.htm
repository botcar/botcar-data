  10. Turdus Rhomboidalis: The Tang.

  11. Turdus Flavus: The Hog-Fish.


  12. Turdus Oculo radiato: The Pudding-Wife.

  13. Mormyrus ex cinero nigricans: The Bone-Fish.

  15. Suillis: The great Hog-Fish.

  19. Unicornis, Piscis Bahamensis: The Bahama Unicorn Fish.

  20. Muraena maculata, nigra & viridis: The Muray.

  21. Muraena Maculata Nigra: Lithophyton longe racemosum cortice flavo persorato: The black Murey.
    Lithophyton longe racemosum cortice flavo persorato.

  22. Turdus Oculo Radiato Guaperva Maxima Caudata: The Old Wife.

  23. Bagre Secunde Specii Marggr. Affinis: The Cat Fish.

  24. Harengus Minor, Bahamensis: The Pilchard.
    Arbor, Populi nigrae foliis; fructu reni-formi mono-spermo.

  25. Anthea quartus Rondeletii Forte etiam Orpheus veterum: The Mutton Fish.
